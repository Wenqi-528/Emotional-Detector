FAQ

What coding language did you use and how long did it take you to build the model?

We used Python to build up the Keras model and created html, css, js files based on the Flask package.  

We took one week and a half.

What different emotions will it detect?

Joy, Anger, Love, Sadness, Fear, and Surprise.

What was your motive to making this emotion recognition bot?

Our motive was to help autistic people and those with anxiety and other disorders to recognize emotions and feel more comfortable in social situations.

Why isn't the bot 100% accurate?

Our model is trained on a specific grammar and only 6 emotions, so it may not recognize some other texts and emotions.
